  2. Bitcoin/USD Weekly chart

    3.   3. ETH/USD Daily Chart

    4. The current price of Ethereum is $315. Ethereum has the second largest market cap after Bitcoin, and it is termed as the next big thing in the blockchain domain. It is following a similar chart pattern as Bitcoin. ETH/USD has been in a good uptrend from its low of $83. From the daily chart, it seems ETH is approaching the 100-weekly Moving Average, an important level of resistance. Apart from that, it has hit the channel high as indicated in the chart. The 100-weekly MA average also coincides with the 23.6% Fib retracement level which is $375. This level of resistance was also seen in June and August of 2017. If the ETH price corrects from here then we may see it come down to following support levels:

    1. S1 - $255
    2. S2 - $223
    3. S3 - $190
